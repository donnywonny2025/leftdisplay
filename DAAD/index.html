FL License Strategy (If Asked at Hearing)

If the hearing officer asks about the Florida license, the answer is simple and honest:

"I moved to Florida and applied for a license. Florida issued one. I drove responsibly on it for years. My FL driving record is clean — no incidents, no violations. My focus today is demonstrating that my substance use is under control."

Do NOT over-explain or apologize. Do NOT volunteer details. Redirect to sobriety. The clean FL driving history supports your case — it proves you're a responsible driver.
